Scrambled Eggs

Eggs were one of my favourute foods.here is a recipe for delociously scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2bs cream

method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over meduium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once the butter has melted and cream and eggs
  4. Using spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omlette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove frome the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served
